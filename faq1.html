FAQ

FAQs make it easier for you to find important information quickly. Discover answers to common questions about our services.

How does the Dyzel menu appear?

The Dyzel menu is adapted from luxurious fine dining menus from various restaurants around the world. It has been transformed into diet-friendly and healthy meals created by top professional chefs in Jakarta, who have extensive experience working in both local and international 5-star restaurants.

Is the nutritional content of Dyzel menus guaranteed?

The menus available at Dyzel are closely monitored and fully supported by the best nutrition specialists and dietitians in Jakarta.

How many customers have successfully subscribed to Dyzel?

Hundreds of thousands of people have successfully lost weight with Dyzel.

What does a Dyzel box contain?

Each Dyzel box contains low-calorie meals, typically around 450-550 calories. The meals include complex carbohydrates, lean animal protein, unsaturated fats, vegetables, and minimal sugar, salt, and saturated fats.

What is DyzelFitness?

DyzelFitness is a comprehensive ebook that provides information on diet and healthy living. It also includes a complete video exercise program suitable for beginners and experts, which can be practiced at home, in the office, or at the gym.

How does the DyzelFitness system work?

Dyzel operates on a subscription basis for one year. If you purchase a package that includes DyzelFitness, you'll receive a special account to access the DyzelFitness website.

What is the pricing for Dyzel packages based on the number of days?

The pricing applies to deliveries spanning 5 to 20 days. If you need additional days on weekends, you can request them separately.

When can I expect Dyzel food deliveries?

Food deliveries are estimated to arrive before 13:00 for lunch and before 18:00 for dinner.
